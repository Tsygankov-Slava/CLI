Usage:
   command [flags] [arguments]

Commands:
  printName                          Displays "Hello 
                                     [entered nam-
                                     e]!". 
	Flags:
      -n, --name=VALUE[REQUIRED]     A flag that 
                                     accepts a 
                                     name as input.
      -s, --surname=VALUE[REQUIRED]  A flag that 
                                     accepts a 
                                     surname for
                                     entry. 
	Example:
      $ printName -n Name
      >>> Hello Name!

  printHello                         Displays the 
                                     word "Hell-
                                     o!". 
	Example:
      $ printHello 
      >>> Hello!
